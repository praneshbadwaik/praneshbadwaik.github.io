About Me

I am an undergrad student at Pennsylvania State University, University Park. My major is Mathematics, with specialization in Computational Mathematics. My interests include Graph Theory, Topology, Big Data Analysis, Functional Programming and Lambda Calculus. Recently, I have been playing around with F# and implementing some Graph Theory stuff in it, which might seem like an unlikely pairing but I am enjoying it so far.

My hobbies include watching football (Go Packers!), tennis, playing PS4 games like BloodBorne and listening to music (Maggie Rogers, Halsey and Ty Segall are my favorites right now).
